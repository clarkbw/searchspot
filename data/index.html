      Welcome to Awesome Search!

      Let's get you setup and using the best search engines possible. Follow these short instructions and you'll be all set to go!

      First

      Click the blue button above to get the Yelp search engine installed. Yelp provides local results for all your searches so if you're looking for restaurants, shops, or parks you'll get quick access to all the address and phone number information you need along with Yelp reviews.

      Then pat yourself on the back

      By default you're participating with our anonymous statistics collection. Your participation helps improve this add-on everyday. The add-on is not collecting personally identifying information but is collecting data about the search engines used and seen.

      If you don't wish to participate you can turn off participation in the Awesome Search add-on preferences.

      Finally

      You're all done. There's nothing else to do but get searching.
